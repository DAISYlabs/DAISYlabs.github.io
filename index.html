Deep artificial intelligent systems for biomedical image processing

DAISYlab is a joint effort of PhD, MSc and MD students of the University Medical Center Hamburg-Eppendorf (UKE) and the Technical University Hamburg (TUHH) to establish a deep learning platform for biomedical image processing that allows efficient collaboration and knowledge exchange between UKE and TUHH working groups in the field of biomedical imaging and image processing. It is funded by Forschungszentrum Medizintechnik Hamburg (fmthh).
